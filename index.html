What to Expect

Finding Us

Our main church center is conveniently located in the heart of New York City. Free parking is available on Sundays at our dedicated lot, with additional parking at the Kingdom Plaza Garage two blocks away. Our ushers will guide you upon arrival to help you find your way.

Our Services

Experience the supernatural in our power-packed services led by our esteemed ministers. Be lifted by the special atmosphere of faith, enlightened by the teaching of God's Word, and transformed by the power of the Holy Spirit. Our services feature anointed praise and worship, life-changing testimonies, and the accurate teaching of the Word.

Loveworld Kids

Loveworld Kids Ministry provides specialized programs for children from 6 weeks through 12 years, teaching them the Word of God in creative and engaging ways. Your children will learn powerful Bible truths, participate in Spirit-filled worship, and build friendships in a safe, loving environment guided by our trained ministry team.

Rhapsody of Realities

Rhapsody of Realities, the world's #1 daily devotional and Bible study guide, has transformed millions of lives worldwide through its revelation of God's Word. Join our distribution and study programs to grow in your faith and help reach others with this life-changing devotional, available in over 7,000 languages and dialects globally.

Healing School

Divine Healing Ministry

The Healing School is a global ministry of Pastor Chris Oyakhilome dedicated to manifesting the healing works of Jesus Christ today. Through powerful healing services, countless testimonies of divine healings and miraculous interventions have been recorded. Experience the demonstration of God's Word and the power of the Holy Spirit as lives are transformed.

Inner-City Mission

Reaching the Unreached

The Inner-City Mission for Children is a faith-based NGO with a vision to reach indigent children and orphans in inner cities around the world. Through our education initiatives, feeding programs, healthcare services, and spiritual nurturing, we're breaking the cycle of poverty and bringing hope to the most vulnerable among us.
