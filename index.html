What to Expect

Getting Here

We are located in the heart of New York, street parking is free on Sundays, and our parking deck is located just a couple blocks away at the Central Plaza Garage on Main St. The parking deck is open from 8a-3p on Sundays.

Gathering Details

Sunday services are a vibrant celebration where you and your family can experience the power of God's Word taught by our ministers, join in Spirit-filled worship, and connect with the global Loveworld family. Our services are life-transforming experiences filled with praise, worship, and impactful teaching at 9:30a and 11:30a every Sunday.

Family Ministries

Our children's church program provides age-appropriate biblical teaching and activities for children from 6 weeks through 5th grade during both morning services. Our youth ministry for middle and high school students meets during the 11:30a service only, helping young people develop a strong foundation in God's Word.
