WHO WE ARE

Loveworld International is a vibrant community centered on Christ's love. We are dedicated to sharing the message of God's grace and transforming lives through the power of His word. Our mission is to bring people into a meaningful relationship with Jesus and create a global impact through love and service.

SUNDAY MORNING

WHAT TO EXPECT

GETTING HERE

We are located in the heart of the city. Street parking is free on Sundays, and our parking deck is located just a couple blocks away.

GATHERING DETAILS

Sunday gatherings are a space for you and your family to join in the fellowship of the people of God, be enriched by the teachings of the Word of God, and lift your voices in joyful song.

FAMILY MINISTRIES

Our family ministries on Sundays are located just a few doors down from the main auditorium. We have a place for children ages 6 weeks through 5th grade during both morning gatherings.
