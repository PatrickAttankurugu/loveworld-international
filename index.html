What to Expect

Getting Here

We are located in the heart of New York, street parking is free on Sundays, and our parking deck is located just a couple blocks away at the Central Plaza Garage on Main St. The parking deck is open from 8a-3p on Sundays.

Gathering Details

Sunday gatherings are a space for you and your family to join in the fellowship of the people of God, be enriched by the teachings of the Word of God, and lift your voices in joyful song to the praises of God. We gather at 9:30a and 11:30a every Sunday at The Life Center.

Family Ministries

Our family ministries on Sundays are located just a few doors down from The Life Center at 123 Faith St. We have a place for children ages 6 weeks through 5th grade during both morning gatherings. Middle and high school students meet during the 11:30a gathering only.
